About Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist, former chairman of Tata Sons, and a prominent philanthropist. He has played a pivotal role in the growth and expansion of the Tata Group.

Achievements

  • Led Tata Group's expansion into international markets.
  • Received numerous awards for his contributions to business and society.
  • Advocated for corporate social responsibility.
